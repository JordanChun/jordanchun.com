Tiger Painting - Digital Art

A digtal photorealistic painted tiger. Achieved with Adobe Photoshop brush techniques.

During the third semester of my program, I completed this artwork in an advanced photoshop course. Although it appears to be painted by hand, it was completed only using a mouse. I wanted to challenge myself with the use of lights and darks to create a photorealistic image. Through the use of the pen tool, customized brushes and many bending and blurring effects, I have achieved something I never thought I could do.

One of the hardest aspects of this assignment was making the fur look believable. After experimenting with many different brushes, I found that making my own brushes achieved the best result.

I attempted to make the whiskers with a simple brush, however it was hard to simulate the fine appearance with just a mouse. I decided to make a custom stroke, and apply it to individually pathed whiskers.
