Chuck Norris Vs Terminator Movie Poster - Graphic Design

A mock movie poster for Chuck Norris Vs Terminator. Created with Photoshop and Illustrator.

This movie poster makes use of a large variety of techniques in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. These techniques include: Layer masking of complex images, creative and custom brush creations and usage, blending modes, typography, and a sense of humour. The meaning of this movie poster was to provide pinch of humour but also a great thrill of action that would take place in the movie itself.
